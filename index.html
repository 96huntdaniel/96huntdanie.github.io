About Me

Hello! I am a Scrum Master at Shelter Insurance, where I help my teams and our business partners deliver quality code and improve our Claims application. I began my career as a support engineer, and after transferring to a Scrum team I fell in love with the empowerment and efficiency that Agile provided. Now my passion is to spread those values and enable teams to become the best that they can be, and provide maximum value to their organization.


Outside of work, I am a husband and soon-to-be father. I love to travel with my wife, spend time with our dogs, and cook. I believe that being a genuine person is just as important to a team's success as any skillset.
